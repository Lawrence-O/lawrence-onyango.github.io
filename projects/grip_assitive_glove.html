Grip Assistive Glove

Spring 2023 (~2 months)

Overview

The Grip Assistive Glove is a glove that was developed to improve grip strength in individuals with conditions such as Cerebral palsy or Tendonitis. We modified a standard glove to help a user grip with a combination of motors, links, and fishing lines. Extensive early and developed ideation led to the creation of a functional prototype, supported by stress analysis and Finite Element Analysis (FEA). Overall, we were able to deliver an affordable, user-friendly assistive glove, estimated to cost approximately $46.30, to significantly improve the quality of life and independence of individuals with limited grip strength.
