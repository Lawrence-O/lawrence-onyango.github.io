Predicting Buggy Freeroll Times

Spring 2023 (~1 month)

Overview

In this project, we undertook the task of employing multivariate linear regression (MVR) to predict freeroll times for buggies by analyzing easily obtainable parameters. We mainly analyzed elements such as air temperature, freeroll initial velocity, and weight since these often changed a buggy's free roll time. We successfully established an equation with an R-squared value of 0.8, demonstrating a strong correlation between these input factors and freeroll time. Buggy racing at CMU is a long-standing tradition, where even the smallest time-saving improvements are crucial, particularly in the freeroll segment. The project's analysis offers valuable insights into the interplay of these parameters, paving the way for future optimizations, expanded data collection, and considerations for more complex factors in the quest for minimal freeroll times in buggy racing.
