Habitat Linkage System

Spring 2023 (~2 months)

Overview

In this project we designed, optimized, and tested a mechanical linkage system, whose aim was to press a button for the longest time while ensuring structural integrity. The project was comprised of intermediate and final testing phases, with initial and final button press times varying due to an initial setup error. Key improvements included filleting hexagonal hole edges to reduce stress concentrations and optimizing mass, leading to a 72.4% reduction. The final system achieved a button press time of 28 seconds, demonstrating structural soundness. Ultimately, this project showcased a rigorous engineering design process, highlighting the importance of material selection, structural analysis, and continuous improvement in achieving an efficient and optimized mechanical linkage system.
